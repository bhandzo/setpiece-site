### Three Tracks Moving the Business at Once

Embedded operators rebuilding how the business runs across stack, org, and the people doing the work. AI is the lever; outcomes are the deliverable.

**Transformation Tracks:**

- **Technical & Data (Ben):** Rebuild the data foundation, modernize legacy technical teams, ship bespoke tooling tailored to your operation.
- **Executive & Org (Kim):** Leadership operating system, decision velocity, organizational design, culture.
- **Team Capability (both):** AI-leveraged workflows for the people doing the work, so productivity actually compounds.

**Pre-requisite:** Strategic clarity from a State-of-Play Audit, or equivalent shared read on where the operation is constrained.
